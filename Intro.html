Emoji God

Modern entertaining metaphysical installation

"How did your exam go today?"

"Seeking a sign!"

In Asian cultural traditions, metaphysics is considered a form of spiritual solace. Modern Asian youth, when faced with stress, uncertainty, or even simple decision-making dilemmas, tend to turn to metaphysics for comfort. In the context of a tech-savvy culture, we have created a contemporary, entertaining form of metaphysics that aligns with the current zeitgeist. Through an emoji-based divination approach, users can interpret their own fortunes in an entertaining way, thereby gaining a harmless form of spiritual solace. We aim to provide a more relatable emotional outlet for young people through this combination of entertainment and emotional expression in modern design.
